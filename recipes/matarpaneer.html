Matarpaneer

Description

Mattar paneer, also known as matar paneer, muttar paneer, and mutter paneer is a modern restaurant style and vegetarian North Indian dish consisting of peas and paneer in a tomato based sauce, spiced with garam masala. It is often served with rice and an Indian type of bread
